About us

Our team is made up of enthusiastic students who are passionate about Internet of Things (IoT) technology and full-stack web development. Our project is an attempt to investigate the limitless possibilities of IoT and its applications in real-world scenarios, as well as its integration into web development.

The Industrial Space Management System is a cutting-edge solution for managing and optimising industrial spaces. With the increasing demand for resource efficiency, our system aims to provide sustainability and security to industrial spaces through remote environmental monitoring and RFID-based access, It involves the use of a wide range of hardware and software and technologies.These include ESP32, ESP32CAM, BME280, MQ-Gas sensors, RFID, Micropython, Arduino C, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Chart.js, Flask, Python, and PostgreSQL, Timescale-DB, among others.
